how to care for string of hearts and why you should get one

--> Light:This plant likes filtered lighting and heat. If inside, place your plant near the brightest window in your home. If outside, opt for a shady spot.

--> Watering: Wait until the soil is completely dried out before you water again. Usually once a week is sufficient

--> Why: String of Hearts are great plants to hang because they grow in vines. They are a great plant to show off since their leaves take the shape of tiny hearts!
