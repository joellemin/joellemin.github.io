how to care for a spider plant and why you should get one

--> Light:Out of all the plants, spider plants enjoy super bright sunlight and can live happily on a windowsill. I have mine hanging in front of a north facing window.

--> Watering: Wait until the soil is completely dried out before you water again. Usually once a week is sufficient. Draining is especially important for the spider plant. If the tips of the leaves are browning, try leaving out your water overnight to lessen the flouride levels.

--> Why: Spider plants have beautiful striped leaves that grow quickly. It also produces white flowers and baby plants on long stems. The happier the plant is, the more flowers and baby plants it produces!
