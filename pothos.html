how to care for pothos and why you should get one

--> Light:Pothos thrive when living indoors. It prefers bright, indirect light near a window. However, pothos can also tolerate low-light conditions. For fast growth, a window will be best

--> Watering: Erratic water care is okay for pothos. Make sure to wait till the soil dries out before your next water.

--> Why: Pothos care is very easy and a great and affordable first plant to have. It is also extremely easy to propogate and grows fast!
